About Us
Techspardha

Techspardha is the Techno-Managerial Festival of NIT Kurukshetra which started in 1995 as "Technospect" (later changed to "Literati"). It has a variety pf events (60+) ranging from paper presentations, roboticsm software development, aero-modelling, quizzes to managerial events. The year 2013 marked the Golden-Jubilee of NIT Kurukshetra and thus the mega techno-managerial festival was re-christened "TECHSPARDHA".
TS'17 attracted participants from more thanm 350 colleges across the country.

A Glimpse into the future

Inspired by the attempts by vanguards to provide ideas and solutions for the betterment of human life Techspardha this time comes with the theme Next Generation Technology, providing you a glimpse into the future.

Guest Lecture

Narasimha Karumanchi

Author of book on DAA and DS which is being followed by students of our institute very rigorously. He is founder of CareerMonk.


Siddharth Rajhans

Siddharth Rajhans is a first generation technology entrepreneur, Silicon Valley technocrat, Management researcher for workspace efficiency and innovation issues, TEDx and motivational speaker at Indian universities and management institutes and a pioneer of the Outernet Technology in India. Siddharth is committed to building a developed India and is contributing his bit by helping bring free internet connectivity in rural Indian areas and scaling-up to Indian cities. Former President of India Late Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was instrumental in appreciating Siddharth's research work on satellite-powered internet.


Zubin Sharma

He is the founder of Project Potential - an NGO which is empowering the untapped potential of India. This Bihar based NGO is developing enterpenueral minds of youngsters to increase employment and development. He is a graduate of University of Pennsylvania. He has another NGO named, Project SEEKHO under which he and his team has taught around 4000 students. A person who is dedicating his life for the development of poor and underprivileged people.


Vivek Atray

An alumni of NIT Kurukshetra batch 1984. He is an inspirational speaker, CEO mentor, independent advisor, author and a former IAS.


Abhas Mitra

One of the most mentioned living Indian Physicists on web.A TEDx Speaker. An Indian Cosmologist who redefined the Black Hole physics and pulled the attention of Physicists at international level. He solved the Black hole information Paradox before the the great Cosmologist Stephen Hawking.
